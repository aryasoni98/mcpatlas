# RFC-NNNN: Short Title

## Summary

One or two sentences describing the change and outcome.

## Context

- Why is this change needed?
- What is the current state?

## Problem

- What specific problem does this solve?
- Who is affected?

## Proposed solution

- What do you propose?
- Key design decisions and tradeoffs.

## Alternatives considered

- Other options and why they were rejected.

## Migration path

- How do we roll this out without breaking existing users?
- Backward compatibility, feature flags, deprecation.

## References

- Links to Blueprint, issues, or prior discussions.
